ABOUT US

Fine Fabrica is a community-driven makerspace at the heart of Villanova, PA. We provide a space for people of all ages, incomes, and talents to collaborate, learn new skills, and come together and make. We’re a community of local engineers, makers, artists, and designers who work together to create a productive and creative environment.

FAQ

What is a makerspace?
A makerspace is a collaborative workshop environment equipped with tools, materials, and resources for individuals to explore and engage in hands-on learning, creativity, and innovation across various disciplines such as electronics, woodworking, 3D printing, and more.
What kind of equipment/tools are available in the makerspace?
 A wide range of equipment is available to members. To find specific tools, look in the “services” section.
Can anyone use the makerspace, or is it limited to certain groups?
 Anyone can use the makerspace. People with memberships have further access to materials and classes. To see age restrictions, see the faq below.
Are there age restrictions for using the makerspace?
 The age limitations vary between classes. Some require individuals to be over 13, 16, or 18 years old, while others have no age restrictions. To check, look at the events on the calendar. Children under 13 should be accompanied by an adult.
Can I bring my own materials to use in the makerspace?
 Yes. As long as none of the materials are harmful, dangerous, or are likely to cause issues.
Are there staff or volunteers available to help me learn how to use the equipment?
 For any classes, teachers will explain how to use tools, and guide you through the process of using them. To rent specific tools, it is recommended to have some knowledge on how to use them.
Can I reserve equipment or workspace in advance?
 Yes. You can find rental options under the “services” section of the website.
What are the operating hours of the makerspace?
 We allow people to rent equipment and space from 6 am to 9 pm. Classes can range at any time of day.
Can I use the makerspace for personal projects, or is it primarily for educational purposes?
 You can use it to learn and improve, for a personal project, or something else.
