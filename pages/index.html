WHAT WE DO

We work together using our shared resources to plan and create new and innovative products, ideas, and designs. We cover and teach a wide range of subjects and skills including science, technology, engineering, and art. Here at Fine Fabrica, we value collaboration as the best way to learn and retain value from our experiences.

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN

You can have fun, be creative, or learn something new at Fine Fabrica. We believe that the joy of making shouldn't be gatekept by high prices and bad systems. Because of this, we have cost effective bookings and 24/7 access for Tier 2 & 3 Members. Tier 3 Members will also get 2% cash back on materials bought at sponsor stores.
